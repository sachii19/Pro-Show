About us


Throughout the world, dog owners engage their dogs in physical acticty, but there is no way they can track their steps.
Dogs are prone to running away or wandering someplace when they hear loud and unfamiliar noises.
But, now worry not, because FurF!t is here.
FurF!t is a device , which you can attach to any collar. It consists of a GPS tracker, footsteps moniter and heart rate moniter. All you have to is download the FurF!t app, and you will be able to keep tabs on your furry friend from anywhere around the world.
